## ðŸ§  KEY CONCEPTS

### Accounting Aphasia
**Definition:** GAAP's systematic failure to recognize intangible investments as assets.  
**Result:** Growth investors appear to be losers. Huge mispricing opportunity.

### The Company C Trap
**Warning:** Most dangerous scenario is GAAP profitable but economically losing.  
**How to Spot:** Declining ROIC, worsening competitive position, "harvesting" past investments.

### Good vs Bad Losses
**Good Loss:** Investing in future (R&D, customer acquisition, brand building)  
**Bad Loss:** Operating inefficiency, value destruction, broken business model  
**The Difference:** Free cash flow potential when intangibles are mature
